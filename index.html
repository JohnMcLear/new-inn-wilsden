About the pub

The New Inn is a traditional village pub with a relaxed, multi‑room feel and plenty of character. Four hand-pulls pour a rotating lineup of cask ales alongside a broad range of lagers, ciders, wines and spirits.

  • Sky & TNT Sports on big screens
  • Two dartboards & games room
  • Large car park & beer garden
  • Dog friendly — water bowls & treats
  • Bar snacks (try the warm pork scratchings!)
